A fast-paced endless runner set in a neon-lit cyberpunk city. The player controls a hacker sprinting through procedurally generated rooftops, dodging obstacles and collecting data fragments to hack into the mainframe.

The gameplay loop is simple: run, jump, slide, collect. But the twist is the "hack mode" — collecting enough data fragments triggers a brief bullet-time sequence where the player can manipulate the environment, removing obstacles or creating shortcuts.

Visual style is retro-futuristic with glowing neon lines on dark backgrounds. Synthwave soundtrack pulses in sync with gameplay speed. The faster you run, the more intense the music becomes, creating an adrenaline feedback loop.

Mobile-first design with swipe controls, but also supports keyboard for desktop play. Leaderboards via localStorage for now, with potential for online leaderboards post-MVP.
