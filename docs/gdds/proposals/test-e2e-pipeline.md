Pixel Bounce is a simple HTML5 Canvas arcade game where the player controls a bouncing ball navigating between platforms. The ball automatically bounces upward and the player uses left/right controls to steer it onto platforms and collect stars scattered across the screen.

The core loop is straightforward: bounce higher, collect stars, avoid falling off the bottom of the screen. Each star collected adds to the score and slightly increases the ball speed, creating a natural difficulty curve. Missing a platform means game over.

The hook is the satisfying bounce physics combined with the pressure of increasing speed. Platforms scroll downward as the player progresses, creating an endless vertical climber feel. The visual style is clean pixel art with bright colors — think simple geometric shapes with a retro arcade aesthetic.

Controls are minimal: arrow keys or touch swipe for horizontal movement. The ball bounces automatically on contact with platforms. Special golden stars occasionally appear that grant a brief slow-motion effect, giving the player a strategic resource to manage during intense sequences.
